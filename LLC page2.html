How I’ve Achieved goals

I have achieved this goals by using different methods and apps.

Gizmo

In Gizmo, we can upload chapter PDFs, create quizzes, and test ourselves every week to remember what we’ve learned. We can also create groups with friends and study or quiz together. There is a “school-wise” section too, and our school, 21K School, is listed, we currently have 50 members using it.
(Want to see Gizmo? Click the image!)

Feynman Technique

I use the Feynman Technique to remember concepts. When we imagine teaching someone, use a soft toy as a student, or explain the topic by writing it down, we understand it much better. This technique helps a lot during assessments.

Century Tech

I use Century Tech to revise the chapter, understand the concepts better, and practice at my own pace. It also gives me badges for my achievements, which keeps me motivated and makes learning more fun. It really helps me check my progress and improve step by step.

AI for Learning

I use ChatGPT and other AIs to test myself by asking them to prepare HOTS and skill-based questions for each chapter. I also check if my answers are correct and ask for suggestions to improve them. We can use AI for learning, practising, and becoming better with every chapter.

Thea

Thea helps me summarize chapters and pick out important points, making it easier to revise quickly and understand concepts clearly.
(Want to experience Thea? Click the image!)

NCERT Worksheets

NCERT provides worksheets for Math and Science, which are extremely useful. They are available for classes 6 to 12.
(Want to experience it? Click the image!)

Notion

I use it to stay organized and complete my assignments. There are many templates available for planning, and I also use the Eisenhower Matrix to prioritize tasks and plan effectively.
