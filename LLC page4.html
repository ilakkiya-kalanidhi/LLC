Habits of Mind I'm aware of

As we are one of the Habits of Mind-certified schools, we are all familiar with the Habits of Mind.

The Habits of Mind I Have Incorporated Are:

Persisting – I keep trying to improve my artwork and Maths, even when it gets challenging.

Managing Impulsivity & Listening with Understanding and Empathy – This works better with my friends than with my sibling 😅, but I’m trying to improve.

Applying Knowledge to New Situations – This habit helps me a lot in Maths.

Gathering Data Through All Senses – During MUN, I went all the way back to 660 BCE Japan and even read the Constitution. I used the tagline “Live with Japan, Eat with Japan, Sleep with Japan.” suggested by Ms. Maitreyee. Ms. Shazia later said it wasn’t needed, I still got a participation certificate 😭✨

Thinking Interdependently – This works 100% when I collaborate with my friends.

Striving for Accuracy – I always try to redo my work and correct my mistakes. My mind simply won’t let me rest until it’s right.

Thinking About Thinking – I think a lot… maybe too much. If thinking could stop a brain, mine would still keep going.

Finding Humor – After serious scolding sessions from Ms. Maitreyee ma’am, Roshan, Ishaanvi, and Sai usually gather around and say, “Rainwater harvesting!” Thanks to them, I always end up laughing again.
