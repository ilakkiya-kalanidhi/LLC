Weakness/ Challenges

1. In Math, I often make silly mistakes while learning new concepts like Algebra and Geometry, even when I understand them.

2. After MUN, keeping up with homework and academics feels overwhelming. I spend a lot of time completing assignments, which can be mentally exhausting. If we have any FA (Formative Assessment) during that time, I use AI to summarize chapters so I can revise faster.

3. When I make a mistake or get scolded, my mind doesn’t let it go easily. I tend to overthink situations until I fix the mistake or explain myself properly. Because of this, I sometimes stress over very small errors. I put a lot of pressure on myself to do things perfectly. During these moments of overthinking, my friend Ishaanvi really supports me and helps calm me down, bringing me back to focus and confidence.

Despite everything, I’m trying to improve, learn from mistakes, and manage my stress better.
