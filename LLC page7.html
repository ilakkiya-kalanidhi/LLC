Acknowledgment

This year, many people supported and guided me, and I would like to thank and acknowledge them.

Parents

I am thankful to my parents for supporting me and for buying the things I need or want. During MUN, they were not very interested at first, but when they heard me speak, they were really happy and proud of how I represented myself. Whenever I create my artworks, they always appreciate and encourage me. This does not mean I don’t have scolding sessions, of course I do, especially regarding Math, screen time, and other things. However, after the LLC, they might understand everything I have done and the efforts I have put in.

Tech Team

Behind every working screen, and every successful event is the tech team. They are the real backbone of our school. Whether it is fixing issues at the last minute, or supporting us whenever we need help, they are always there without hesitation. Their hard work often goes unnoticed, but without them, many of our ideas and even our school would never turn into reality. The tech team ensures everything runs smoothly, allowing students and teachers to focus on learning and creativity. They are truly the silent heroes of our school!

21K School

21K School is the best school I could have asked for. Without it, I don’t know where I would be today. The freedom to explore, learn, and be myself has helped me grow in ways I never imagined. Here, we are encouraged to try new things, express our ideas, and follow our passions without limits. I truly love how flexible and supportive the environment is. And honestly… if there is ever a 21K University in the future, I would 100% join! no second thoughts!
